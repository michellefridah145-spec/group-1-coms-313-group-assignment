Understanding Mental Health Physically

Mental health is closely linked to physical well-being. Stress, anxiety, and depression can manifest in the body through fatigue, headaches, muscle tension, and weakened immunity. Taking care of your physical health through exercise, nutrition, and rest supports emotional balance and resilience.
